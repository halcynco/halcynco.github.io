About

Halcyon Partners is a consulting firm founded by engineers and researchers who spent nearly a decade building production systems at the intersection of large-scale data processing, statistical modelling, and quantitative finance.

We specialise in architecting scalable systems for processing unstructured data. Our designs are built for durability: minimal operational overhead, few moving parts, and low ongoing maintenance. The result is infrastructure that stays out of your way and holds up under real-world load for years.

Services

End-to-end architecture for ingesting, transforming, and serving unstructured data at scale — designed to run with minimal intervention.

System Design

High-throughput, low-latency pipelines built on simple, composable components that are easy to reason about and maintain.

ML & Statistical Modelling

Production-grade modelling infrastructure — from feature engineering to deployment — with a focus on reproducibility and reliability.
